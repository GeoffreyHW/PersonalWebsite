My name is Geoffrey Wong. I am a junior studying Computer Science at Arizona State University. Outside of classes, I TA for CSE 100: Principles of Programming with C++, am Vice President of both the ASU chess and Quizbowl club, and do research for Meteor Studios at ASU.

My research topic is studying the energy efficiency of augmented-reality platforms. This involves characterizing beneficial tradeoffs between performance and other factors such as speed, camera configurations, marker size, etc. I hope to lay groundwork so that future augmented-reality experiences feel more vibrant and immersive.
